Frequently Asked Questions

How do I donate?
You can donate via M-Pesa, PayPal, Crypto, or Bank Transfer. Visit our Donate page for details.
How do I know my money is being used properly?
We provide full transparency with regular updates, impact reports, and financial breakdowns.
Can I start a fundraiser for someone?
Yes! You can start a personal fundraiser for a dental patient in need. Visit our Fundraising page for steps.
How do investments work in PhillHealthy?
We allocate 30% of funds into sustainable investments (SACCOs, MMFs, Crypto) to ensure long-term charity growth.
Why did you create PhillHealthy?
PhillHealthy was created to make dental care accessible to those who can't afford it, bridging the gap in healthcare.
What drives PhillHealthy?
Our passion for helping communities, ensuring everyone has access to proper dental care.
Who are your partners?
We are working with donors, dental hospitals, and investors to expand our reach.
Will I benefit from donating?
While the main goal is charity, donors receive updates on their impact and potential tax benefits.
Where are you located?
We are based in Kenya and operate primarily through our online platform.
How does PhillHealthy choose patients who need dental help?
Patients are selected based on their financial need and the severity of their dental issues. We prioritize those who are unable to afford treatment and have no other means of support.
How does PhillHealthy ensure the donations go to the right cause?
We maintain full transparency by tracking and reporting every donation, and funds are sent directly to accredited dental clinics and professionals treating our patients.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, we respect your privacy and allow anonymous donations. You can choose to remain anonymous when donating.
Are the donations tax-deductible?
Yes, donations may be tax-deductible depending on your location and tax laws. Please consult a tax professional for further details.
How can I apply for help from PhillHealthy?
You can apply for help through our website by submitting an application form detailing your dental needs and financial situation.
What dental treatments are covered by PhillHealthy?
We cover a range of dental treatments including, but not limited to, cleanings, extractions, fillings, root canals, and braces.
How long does it take to receive help after applying?
We process applications within 1-2 weeks, but the time may vary depending on the urgency of your case and available funds.
How can I volunteer with PhillHealthy?
We welcome volunteers! You can sign up on our website to assist with outreach, event planning, or even providing dental services if you’re a qualified professional.
Can I contribute dental products to PhillHealthy?
Yes, we accept donations of dental products such as toothbrushes, toothpaste, and other hygiene products to distribute to those in need.
Do you only help people in specific locations?
Currently, we focus on areas where we can have the most impact. However, we are expanding our outreach to other regions. Please check our website for updates.
Is PhillHealthy a non-profit organization?
Yes, PhillHealthy is a registered non-profit organization, focused on improving dental care access for those in need.
Can I sponsor a specific patient’s treatment?
Yes, you can choose to sponsor an individual’s dental treatment. You will be updated on their progress and the impact of your donation.
How can I track the impact of my donation?
We provide regular updates through our website, newsletters, and email about how donations are being used and the difference they’re making.
How do I know if my donation was received?
You will receive an acknowledgment email or a receipt, and you can track your donation through your account on our website.
Do you accept dental insurance?
PhillHealthy operates primarily on donations, but we also work with some dental clinics that may accept insurance for specific treatments.
How can I spread the word about PhillHealthy?
You can help by sharing our website, donating, volunteering, or hosting local fundraising events. Every little bit helps!
What happens if a patient doesn’t use the funds for treatment?
We closely monitor all donations to ensure that funds go directly to the dental services needed. If there is any misuse, the funds are redirected to other patients in need.
