prevent dental cavities

To prevent dental cavities, practice good oral hygiene by brushing teeth at least twice daily with fluoride toothpaste. Floss regularly to remove plaque, limit sugary foods and drinks, and visit your dentist for regular check-ups. Drinking water and using fluoride treatments can also strengthen enamel and prevent decay.

Maintaining Healthy Gums as You Age

As you age, maintaining healthy gums becomes crucial for overall oral health. Regular brushing, flossing, and dental check-ups help prevent gum disease, which can lead to tooth loss. Eating a balanced diet, avoiding smoking, and using fluoride toothpaste also support gum health, keeping your smile bright and strong.
